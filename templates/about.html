About

This website was created by the SAIL lab at Florida Atlantic University's (FAU) Harbor Branch Oceanographic Insitute (HBOI). It serves as a centralized platform for displaying aquaculture data collected by the HAUCS system.

SMS Alert Notifications

In addition to viewing data displayed on this website, you can sign up for real-time alerts over SMS text messages. These messages are triggered when a pond is measured with a dissolved oxygen reading below 30% saturation. The same alert is repeated every 2 hours until the dissolved oxygen rises above 30%.

OPT-IN

To subscribe for SMS based alerts, please fill out the following form with your name and number you wish to subscribe to the alert system. By selecting the subscribe option on the form, you are consenting to receive automated alert messages as described above. Messaging data rates may apply.

OPT-OUT

You can revoke your consent and unsubscribe anytime from this alert system by filling out the same form and selecting unsubscribe. You may also unsubscribe by replying to the SMS messages with "STOP".
